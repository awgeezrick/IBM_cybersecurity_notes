Cybersecurity Associations
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


A list of the most important cybersecurity industry associations
can be found here:
https://cybersecurityventures.com/cybersecurity-associations/#home/?view_1_page=1

Some noteworthy associations are:

- WiCys
    Women in Cybersecurity
    https://www.wicys.org/resources/menu

- SANS
    https://www.sans.org/critical-security-controls/

- OWASP
    OWASP https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Category:OWASP_Project#tab=Project_Inventory

- ISSA
    Information Systems Security Association (ISSA) https://www.issa.org/

- FIRST 
    Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams (FIRST) You will also
    want to review the frameworks available from FIRST. The Frameworks
    are to assist organizations in building, maintaining, and growing
    capabilities of their CSIRT (computer security incident response team
    or PSIRTs (Product Security Incident Response Team)

    These frameworks will be explored further.
    https://www.first.org/education/service-framework


- Additional Cybersecurity Industry Associations
    Examine the list of additional Cybersecurity Industry Associations
    provided by Cybersecurity Ventures.

    https://cybersecurityventures.com/cybersecurity-associations/#home/?view_1_page=1

    Cybersecurity Ventures is the home for the Cybercrime Magazine which
    will give you some of the latest information about what is happening
    in cybersecurity today. Check out the latest issue and explore some of
    the articles and information to get you ready for knowing the latest and
    greatest information around cybersecurity. You will want to take a look
    now and then again at the end of this course to understand important
    topics reviewed during this introductory course in cybersecurity.

    Read this interesting articles on cybersecurity predictions: "Top 5
    Cybersecurity Facts, Figures, Predictions, And Statistics For 2019
    To 2021"

    https://cybersecurityventures.com/top-5-cybersecurity-facts-figures-predictions-and-statistics-for-2019-to-2021/

    As your skills grow in cybersecurity, you will want to bookmark all of
    these resources to keep your skills up to date in this every changing
    world of cybersecurity. You will learn something new everyday!
